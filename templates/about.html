Our Story

Padre Garcia Polytechnic College (PGPC) is a premier institution of higher learning located in the heart of Padre Garcia, Batangas. Established with a vision to provide accessible and quality education to the youth of the region, PGPC has grown into a respected educational institution known for its commitment to academic excellence, character formation, and community service.

Our college was founded on the principles of integrity, innovation, and social responsibility. We believe that education is not just about imparting knowledge, but about developing well-rounded individuals who can contribute meaningfully to society. Through our diverse academic programs and dedicated faculty, we strive to nurture the talents and potentials of every student who walks through our doors.

At PGPC, we foster an environment of learning that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with our student-centered approach, ensure that our graduates are not only academically competent but also morally upright and globally competitive.
