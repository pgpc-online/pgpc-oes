Program Overview

The BS Criminology program at PGPC equips students with knowledge and skills in criminal law, law enforcement, and investigation techniques. Students gain practical experience in forensic science, crime scene analysis, and criminal behavior studies.

What You'll Learn

  • Criminal Law and Procedures
  • Forensic Science and Crime Scene Investigation
  • Law Enforcement Techniques
  • Criminal Behavior and Psychology
  • Traffic and Patrol Operations
  • Community Policing
  • Criminal Investigation and Case Management
  • Physical Fitness and Self Defense

Career Opportunities

  • Police Officer
  • Criminal Investigator
  • Forensic Analyst
  • Correctional Officer
  • Customs and Border Protection Officer
  • Probation/Parole Officer
  • Law Enforcement Trainer

Why Choose Criminology at PGPC?

✓ Modern forensic labs
✓ Experienced law enforcement faculty
✓ Internship in police agencies
✓ Focus on ethics, discipline, and leadership
✓ Community-based learning programs

Admission Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Passing entrance exam
  • Interview with program coordinator
  • Medical and physical fitness requirements
